SERVICES

Freeway provides a network of primarily next-day parcel delivery coverage throughout our direct service territory.

We service your parcel shipments via a diverse and dynamic operating infrastructure which includes multiple vehicle types (cargo vans, sprinter vans, straight trucks, and tractors), static conveyor systems, flexible territorial borders, state-of-the-art scanners, and customizable IT solutions.

Having a courier service that you can trust is essential in today’s fast paced business world.

Courier services are an integral part of our history and growth. We have developed highly effective operations for our customers which uniquely positions us to create a specific solution to meet your organization’s evolving needs. Our combined experience and flexibility allow Freeway to offer high-value, time-definite delivery services to various industries that require courier capabilities. Freeway combines speed with professional service to bring you the solutions you need when you need them.

One of Freeway’s integral services is residential consumer deliveries.

Advancements in consumer technology as well as the rapid growth of e-commerce has revolutionized expectations with regard to package delivery and visibility. Real-time tracking-and-tracing has become the market standard. Freeway is continually investing in the right balance of resources and technology to meet these evolving service standards. We boast an integrated network of real-time scanners and customized delivery software that provides consumers with the visibility they demand.

At Freeway, we pride ourselves on our flexible approach toward the changing needs of the marketplace.

This means our drivers and fleet are equipped to handle more than just standard parcel and courier business. Given the ongoing capacity constraints in the LTL industry, shippers are requiring alternatives to traditional LTL carriers. Freeway is currently providing successful mode-shift options to shippers and is positioned well to continue to meet this market demand.

Freeway leverages a network of operational warehousing facilities within the major metro areas of our service territory.

Because warehousing of goods is often a vital link in the supply chain of many shippers, we are positioned to offer warehouse space as a value-added service to our customers. As a Freeway customer, you gain competitive advantage in your business when your transportation needs require additional time and space.
